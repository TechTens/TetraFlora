Tetra Flora is a local boutique in Denver, Colorado. We Specialize in rare and exotic plants,
while educating our customers on how to care for any type of house plant.
We don’t sell plants online because we believe the plant shopping experience should be as personal as possible, we want you to browse our inventory and come in to be sure the plant you’re interested is the best option for you!
If not, no worries, our highly specialized team can help you find the best options.
